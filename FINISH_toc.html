Podcast Finish

The test cases in this category are used to verify the requirements associate with the completion or early termination of a play.

  1. FINISH-0010 - When a podcast completes, the main menu is presented - This test case is obsolete - requirement SFWIAUDIO-PR-FINISHED-010 has been removed.
  2. FINISH-0020 - Pressing back while podcast is playing, terminates play
  3. FINISH-0030 - When a podcast completes, the play menu becomes dormant
