Test Case STARTUP-0020
Title Application startup with podcasts in the podcast directory
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the app is started by selecting from the Android app selection menu, the app shall do the following:
  • If podcasts do exist in the directory, the app shall display a list of the podcasts that can be played.
  • The app shall require no special provisioning however the podcast settings may be required to be adjusted by the Android OS.
  • sfwiAudio shall display and play only podcasts that are in the podcast directory.
  • sfwiAudio shall have a main menu that displays a list of podcasts available for play.

When the podcast directory has podcast files, the podcasts will be displayed in a list.
Setup
  • App is installed on an Android phone
  • Podcasts are in the podcast directory of the phone
Actions
  1. Start the application.
  2. Verify a list of podcasts are displayed that match the contents of the podcast directory.
Expected The main menu will present a list of podcasts.
Results UNTESTED
Cleanup
  • Unnecessary
Requirements SFWIAUDIO-PR-INIT-010, SFWIAUDIO-PR-INIT-010.002, SFWIAUDIO-PROV-010, SFWIAUDIO-EXT-IF-010, SFWIAUDIO-EXT-IF-020
