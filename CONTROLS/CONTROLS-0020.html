Test Case CONTROLS-0020
Title Pressing Resume, resumes audio play
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the user presses the resume button, the audio shall be resumed from the paused location and the button label shall be changed to pause.
  • When the user presses the pause button, the audio shall be paused and the button label shall be modified to resume.

When the resume button is pressed, audio will resume from the point at which it was paused.
Setup
  • Install app on Android phone
  • Install podcasts into the podcast directory of the phone
  • Start the app
Actions
  1. From the main menu of the app, select a podcast.
  2. Confirm the audio begins to play.
  3. Press the pause button.
  4. Confirm the audio stops and the progress bar is stationary.
  5. Press the resume button.
  6. Verify the audio resumes.
  7. Verify that the audio is playing from the position it was paused at.
  8. Verify that the progress bar is moving.
Expected Audio will resume playing when resume is pressed. The audio will resume from the position it was stopped at.
Results UNTESTED
