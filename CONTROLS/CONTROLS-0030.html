Test Case CONTROLS-0030
Title Slider changes audio position
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the user slides the slider, the audio playback position shall be adjusted accordingly and audio shall play from that adjusted position.

By sliding the slider, this test case illustrates that the playing position can be moved forward and backward in time.
Setup
  • Application installed on an Android phone
  • Podcasts are in the phone's podcast directory
Actions
  1. Start the application.
  2. Select a podcast to play.
  3. On the new menu displayed, move the slider forward.
  4. Verify that the audio appears to play from a different position.
  5. Move the slider to the beginning.
  6. Verify that the audio is what was played when the podcast was initially selected.
  7. Let the podcast play for a while and note the position of the slider's farthest advance.
  8. Move the slider back in time.
  9. Verify that the audio presented has been played before.
  10. Move the slider a bit ahead (but not ahead of the noted position).
  11. Verify that the audio has been played.
Expected Slider movement changes where the audio resumes play.
Results PASSED
Cleanup
  • Unnecessary
Requirements SFWIAUDIO-PR-SLIDER-010
