Test Case CONTROLS-0010
Title Pressing Pause, pauses audio
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the user presses the pause button, the audio shall be paused and the button label shall be modified to resume.

During this test case, the pause if pressed to confirm that the app will pause audio that is playing.
Setup
  • Install the app on an Android phone
  • Load podcasts onto the Android phone that has the app installed
  • Start the app form the may Android display
Actions
  1. From the main menu, select a podcast.
  2. Confirm a new display appears with a progress bar and a pause button.
  3. Confirm the audio is playing.
  4. Press the pause button.
  5. Verify the audio stops and the progress bar pauses.
  6. Verify that the button label changes to resume.
  7. Press the resume button.
  8. Verify that the audio resumes and the progress bar show audio progress.
Expected Pressing the pause button will pause audio and pressing it again will resume audio.
Results UNTESTED
Cleanup
