Test Case FINISH-0030
Title When a podcast completes, the play menu becomes dormant
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the podcast completes, the menu button and slider will no longer affect podcast play.

Letting the podcast play to completion should cause the app to revert to the initial display menu.
Setup
Actions
  1. From the main menu, select a podcast.
  2. Let the podcast play to completion.
  3. Verify the slider bar is all the way to the right.
  4. Verify the button does not pause or resume play.
  5. Verify that the slider does not induce any sort of play action.
  6. Press the back key/button.
  7. Verify the play menu exits and the play list is presented.
Expected When the podcast completes, the play menu will become dormant and pressing the back key will present the play list.
Results PASSED
