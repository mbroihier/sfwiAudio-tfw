Test Case SELECT-0010
Title Selecting a podcast from a list
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When a podcast is selected by pressing a list entry, a new menu will be displayed presenting a pause button and slider, and the podcast will begin playing.
  • sfwiAudio shall have a sub menu that has a pause button and a slider bar that allows for the pausing and resumption of the podcast and adjustment of the playback position.

By pressing a podcast name from a list, this case demonstrates that a podcast is selected for play.
Setup
  • App is installed on Android phone
  • Podcasts are in the podcast directory of the phone
Actions
  1. From the main menu, select the first podcast in the list.
  2. Verify a play menu is displayed with a progress bar and a pause button.
  3. Verify that the audio matches the podcast selected.
Expected The intended podcast is presented in the play menu and is played to the user.
Results PASSED
Cleanup
  • Unnecessary
Requirements SFWIAUDIO-PR-SELECT-010, SFWIAUDIO-EXT-IF-030
